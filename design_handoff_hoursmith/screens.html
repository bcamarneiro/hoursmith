Section 03 — Screens

The recommended direction, on the two screens that matter.

Forge identity, Ledger numerics, Daylight voice — applied to the marketing Home and the working Dashboard. These are faithful redesigns of the real layouts, answering the findings from Section 01. Hour figures are set in tabular mono; ember owns the brand while green / amber / red stay reserved for data meaning.

Answers F5
One clear job

A single dark "close the week" panel leads. The six flat buttons become a hierarchy: one primary action, exports demoted, niche tools tucked into a menu.

Answers F4 · F6
Data colors mean data

Bars and chips use only green/amber/red for status. Ember is reserved for brand and the one action that matters. Every hour is tabular mono.

Answers F3 · Structure
Personal scope, gap-first

Strictly your week — chasing teammates moves to Reports. Your missed day rises to the top with context ("last Thursday you logged…") and a one-click fix.
